532//!
533//! @details
534//! QP::QXMutex is a blocking mutual exclusion mechanism that can also apply

536//! (if initialized with a non-zero ceiling priority, see QXMutex::init()).
537//! In that case, QP::QXMutex requires its own uinque QP priority level,
538//! which cannot be used by any thread or any other QP::QXMutex.
539//! If initialized with preemption-ceiling of zero, QXMutex does **not**
540//! use the priority-ceiling protocol and does not require a unique QP
